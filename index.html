Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our awards program

Who can enter the Beauty & Wellness Awards?

Any brand with products in the beauty, skincare, wellness, or personal care categories can enter. Products must contain at least 90% natural ingredients and adhere to our sustainability standards.

What is the judging process?

Our panel of experts evaluates each product based on our comprehensive criteria, including ingredients, efficacy, sustainability, user experience, and innovation. The process takes 4-6 weeks from submission to final decision.

When will winners be announced?

Winners are announced quarterly, with submission deadlines on January 15, April 15, July 15, and October 15. Winners are notified 2 weeks before public announcement.

How can shoppers trust your awards?

We maintain strict transparency in our judging process. All judges are industry experts with no financial ties to participating brands. Our criteria are publicly available, and we provide detailed feedback to all entrants.

Where can I purchase award-winning products?

All award-winning products are available for purchase through the brands' websites or authorized retailers. We provide direct links to purchase pages on our product showcase section.
